5. What is your impression of the intended primary goal of the interactive experience?

The main purpose of this website is to educate users about the importance of essential jobs, particularly janitorial work. The site aims to help users deeply understand the value of these professions and encourage them to express gratitude for the hard work of those who perform them. Through an interactive experience, the website seeks to break down social prejudices, promote responsible behavior, and foster appreciation and respect for people in these roles.

6. How does the interactive experience communicate this primary goal?

The website is structured with a top navigation bar and a simple scrolling feature, which allows users to focus on the content without unnecessary distractions. The straightforward design helps users immerse themselves in the core message of the site, with the story gradually unfolding through stop-motion animation as they scroll. This sequential storytelling method enables users to naturally grasp the importance of essential jobs as they follow the content's flow. The scene where a cigarette butt is thrown on the ground particularly resonated with me, deepening my connection with the website's message.

7. What is your impression of how the experience should be interacted with over time?

This website seems designed for a single visit, delivering a powerful message in a short amount of time, without requiring repeated visits. One visit should be enough for users to fully understand the site's purpose and develop a sense of awareness about the importance of these professions. The use of stop-motion animation focuses on providing a short, intense experience rather than promoting long-term interaction, aiming to create a strong impact in a brief interaction.

8. How does the interactive experience communicate how it should be interacted with over time?

This website emphasizes a single visit. The stop-motion animation and collage-like visuals immediately immerse the user, and the site is designed to leave a lasting impression with just one experience. By scrolling down, the content naturally unfolds, ensuring that users fully experience the site during their initial visit. The narrative is clearly divided so that users can consume all the content and understand the message completely on their first visit. As a result, the content is designed to be thoroughly experienced without needing additional visits.

Like Hollow - An Interactive Documentary, The Boat - SBS, and Every Last Drop, it uses strong visual imagery and gradually unfolding stories to encourage users to take their time and think deeply about the content.
